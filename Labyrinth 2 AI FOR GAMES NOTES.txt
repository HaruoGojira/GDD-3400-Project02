Labyrinth 2 AI FOR GAMES NOTES


Style - 4 enemies like Pacman ghosts. Each with Unique Style


1. First Enemy: Sniper

- Will move to a corner of the maze and camp at a certain spot
- When it spots the player in Raycast line of sight, it shoots him
- If player doesn't show up within a certain time frame, moves spots until further notice
- Moves by rotating to another corner of the map

2. Second Enemy: Tank

- Slowest of the enemies but is the most tankey (might be indestructible)
- He free roams and is a wander type enemy
- If spots the player he can chase him until player out of sight at a certain time
- He is so big the player can't get trapped in a room or else he dies

3. Third Enemy: Speedster

- fasted of the enemies but is the weakest
- He tries to run from the player when he is spotted
- He can only be beaten by traps and if cornered by the player
- He free roams until spotted by player and then tries to run to another side of the map

4. Fourth Enemy: Hunter

- Medium intelligence as he wanders the map
- When he spots the player, he will try to run at him
- He can leave traps depending on where the player went or last saw him
- Has a medium amount of health and can be killed by traps
